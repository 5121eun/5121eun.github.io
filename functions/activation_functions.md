# Activation Functions

## Sigmoid

$$ 0 \leq x \leq 1 $$

$$
\begin{aligned}

sigmoid(x) =  \frac{1}{1-e^{-1}}

\end{aligned}
$$

## Tanh

$$ -1 \leq x \leq 1 $$

$$
\begin{aligned}

tanh(x) = \frac{sin(x)}{cos(x)} = \frac{e^{2x} - 1}{e^{2x} + 1}

\end{aligned}
$$

## Relu

$$ 0 \leq x  $$

$$
\begin{aligned}

relu(x) = max(0, x)

\end{aligned}
$$
