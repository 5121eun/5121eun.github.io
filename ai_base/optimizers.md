# Optimizers

## SGD(Stochastic Gradient Descent)

$$
\begin{aligned}

W \leftarrow W - \eta \frac{\partial L}{\partial W}

\end{aligned}
$$

## Momentum

$$
\begin{aligned}

v \leftarrow av - \eta \frac{\partial L}{\partial W}


\end{aligned}
$$


$$
\begin{aligned}

W \leftarrow W + v

\end{aligned}
$$
